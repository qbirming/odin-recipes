Gluten Free Lemon Bars

Description

Easy to make gluten-free lemon bars made with fresh lemon. This recipe will will be heavy on the crust and light on the filling.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1 cup Gluten-free Flour
  • 1/3 cup Butter
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 4 tablespoons Half and Half

For the lemon layer:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Gluten-free Flour
  • 2 teaspoons Fresh Shredded Lemon Zest
  • 3 tablespoons Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon Gluten-free Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Position rack in the center of the oven. Line an 8" x 8" x 2" pan with parchment paper; set aside.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, beat the 1/3 cup butter with an electric mixer for 30 seconds. Add 1/4 cup of sugar. Mix until combined.
  3. Add 1 cup of flour and 4 tablespoons half and half. Mix until crumbly.
  4. Press the flour mixture into the bottom of the 8" x 8" x 2" cooking pan. Bake in the oven for 18-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. In a medium mixing bowl, combine 2 eggs, 3/4 cup sugar, 1/4 cup flour, 2 tablespoons lemon zest, and 3 tablespoons baking powder. Beat for one minute on high. Pour over hot baked crust.
  6. Bake an additional 20 minutes, or until light brown and the center is set. Cool at room temperature on a wire rack.
  7. Refridgerate for an hour before cutting into bars.
