Gluten Free Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Sweet, savory, and packed with delicious flavor only using gluten-free ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup Gluten Free Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 2 tablespoons Packed Ligth Brown Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar
  • 1/4 teaaspoon Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 2 teaspoons Peeled and Minced Fresh Ginger
  • 2 teaspoons Peeled and Minced Fresh Garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Onion Powder
  • 2 teaspoons Cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 ibs Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast, cut into 1 ince pieces

Steps

  1. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together all the ingredients except the chicken.
  2. Heat oil in a 12-inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the chicken over medium heat until brown, and the internal temperature reaches at least 160 degrees.
  4. Add the sauce to the cooked chicken and continue to cook and toss until the sauce thickens, about 1 minute.
